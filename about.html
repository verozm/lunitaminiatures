About Lunita Miniatures

Lunita Miniatures began as a hobby. I had been wanting to start working creatively a few years back, but it all began to take shape in 2015 when it truly turned into a passion for crafts. In looking for perfection, I dedicate a lot work to each piece.

Lunita Miniatures is a project for all miniature collectors, for those who always dreamed about having a doll house with everything in it. These miniatures are made with wood, polymer clay, acrylic and more. Everything is made to scale and finely detailed.

My idea is to introduce a passion for miniatures in Mexico, and to compete at an international level with the best miniature creators in Germany, Holland, and England, to mention a few. Every day I try to create original and detailed pieces for L.M. The scale used is 1:12 which is common for this art form.
